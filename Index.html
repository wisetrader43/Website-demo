About the Company

A crew that treats your address like a job number worth getting right.

Euro Canadian Construction Corp. is a Vancouver-based construction company handling residential and commercial work across the Lower Mainland — from ground-up custom homes to a single repainted wall.

Every project starts on paper: a complete project analysis, a floor plan, a financial plan you can actually hold to. Then it moves to site, where the same crew that quoted the job builds it, coat by coat, room by room.

No subcontracted guesswork, no surprise change orders — just a plan, a schedule, and a company that answers the phone.

How a Project Runs

From Sketch to Sign-Off
